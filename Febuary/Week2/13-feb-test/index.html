Lata Mangeshkar (1929-2022)

A Short Biography of Lata Mangeshkar

  1. Lata Mangeshkar, (born September 28, 1929, Indore, British India—died February 6, 2022, Mumbai, India)

  2. Lata Mangeshkar, also known as the 'Nightingale of India', was one of the most versatile singers in the Indian film industry.

  3. Mangeshkar’s father, Dinanath Mangeshkar, was a noted Marathi stage personality popularly known as Master Dinanath.

  4. In a career spanning nearly eight decades, Lata Mangeshkar was the singing voice for a bevy of Bollywood leading ladies.

  5. Songs like Gata Rahe Mera Dil, and Piya Tose in Guide (1965) were recorded for S D Burman.

  6. Lata Mangeshkar received several awards and accolades during her eight-decade-long career. In 1974, she became the first Indian to perform at the Royal Albert Hall.
